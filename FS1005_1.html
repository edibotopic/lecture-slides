Science & Nature

Many scientists consider "natural" to apply to all things equally.
Increasingly, however, some consider it a useful design concept.
In biomimetics nature is used as an inspiration for human-led design
Infant formula is a biomimetic technology

Science & Purity

"Purity" often has a specialised meaning in science.
Legally, it is a concern because of adulteration and authenticity.
However, it is often falsely connected to ideas like beauty

CONCLUSION

Terms like 'natural' and 'pure' are not forbidden words for scientists


but you need to think clearly about their meaning and the context of their use
