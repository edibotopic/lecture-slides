Preserving Natural Structures with Cross-flow Filtration

Cross-flow Filtration

To make acid casein the proteins must be chemically modified before filtration. If smaller-pore filters are used it is possible to recover proteins without causing these modifications. However, to push liquid through these filters greater pressure and turbulence is required.
