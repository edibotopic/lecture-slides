Double Distillation

  1. Wash distilled to produce "low wines"

  2. Low wines cut into heads, heart and tails

    3. Heart = Spirit

Triple Distillation

  1. Wash distilled to produce "low wines"
  2. Low wines distilled to "strong feints"
  3. Strong feints distilled into heart

    4. Heads and feints recycled in 2nd and 3rd still
